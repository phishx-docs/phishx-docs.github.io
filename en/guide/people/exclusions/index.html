# Exclusions

Exclusions and your subscription

The deleted contacts do not count in your subscription and do not even appear in the results.
You can request new exclusions from people who left your company, releasing your subscription to new people.

Manage contacts that have been deleted in the environment.

If you have additional questions, we can make a conference call or send an e-mail to Customer Success.

  1. Access the menu People -> Exclusions.
