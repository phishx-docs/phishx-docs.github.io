# Authenticate

User ID

You can share a single authentication address for your campaign and allow each user without an account or email to access your content by identifying themselves with an ID such as a badge or other unique record, including secure authentication in your environment.
If you need additional support talk to PhishX Support.

View the campaign with the public address that requires user authentication.

  1. Access the menu Campaigns -> Campaigns.

  2. In the Campaign menu.

    Click Authenticate.

    Choose the Authentication option available in your environment.
