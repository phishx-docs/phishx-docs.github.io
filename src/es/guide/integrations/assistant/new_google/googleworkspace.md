# Google Gmail - Configurações Google Workspace

::: tip Replicação para dispositivos
Após a instalação de um suplemento, ele aparecerá nos dispositivos clientes web e mobile em algumas horas.
:::

## Configurações

1. Acessar o **Google Workspace Marketplace Apps** em: [Google Workspace Marketplace Apps](https://admin.google.com/ac/apps/gmail/marketplace/apps)

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_100.png)

   Clicar em **INSTALL APP**.

2. Em **Google Workspace Marketplace**, procurar por **PhishX Assistant**. [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace)

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_102.png)

   Clicar em **PhishX Assistant**. [PhishX Assistant](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/phishx_assistant/308954502633)

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_103.png)

   Clicar em **Admin Install**.

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_104.png)

   Clicar em **CONTINUE**.

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_105.png)

   Em **Allow data access**:

   Verificar as permissões necessárias.

   Em **Install the app automatically for the following users**, selecionar **Everyone at your organization**.

   Marcar **I agree to the application's Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Google Workspace Marketplace's Terms of Service**.

   Clicar em **FINISH**.

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_106.png)

   Clicar em **DONE**.

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_107.png)

3. Acessar novamente o **Google Workspace Marketplace Apps** em: [Google Workspace Marketplace Apps](https://admin.google.com/ac/apps/gmail/marketplace/apps)

   ![Google Assistant](https://cdn.phishx.io/phishx-docs/images/google_workspace_assistant_101.png)

   Verificar o status e distribuição do **PhishX Assistant**.

   **OBS: Aguardar as horas informadas de replicação pela Google**.
